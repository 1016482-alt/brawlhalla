Legends

Yumiko

Yumiko is a mystical legend who uses powerful spirit magic to control the battlefield. She fights with a Bow and a Hammer, giving her a mix of long‑range attacks and heavy‑hitting strikes. Yumiko’s unique abilities let her summon ghostly projectiles and traps that confuse opponents and create space. She is great for players who enjoy creative playstyles and controlling the pace of the match.

QUEEN NAI

Queen Nai is a powerful and defensive legend who uses a Spear and Katars. She has high defense, which makes her harder to knock out, and her attacks hit with strong force. Her spear gives her long reach for controlling space, while her katars allow fast, close‑range pressure. Queen Nai is great for players who like a mix of strong hits, survivability, and steady control over the battlefield.

BODVAR

Bodvar is one of the most balanced and beginner‑friendly legends in Brawlhalla. He uses a Sword and a Hammer, giving him a mix of reliable combos and powerful knockout attacks. Bodvar’s sword provides quick, safe moves, while his hammer delivers heavy hits that can finish opponents early. His balanced stats make him a great all‑rounder for both new and experienced players.
