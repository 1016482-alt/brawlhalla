Useful Tips when playing Brawlhalla

Each weapon in Brawlhalla has its own strengths, weaknesses, combos, and playstyle. Learning how each weapon works helps players improve their movement, spacing, and damage output.

Combos

Practice true combos in Training Mode daily.

Weapon Throws

Throw weapons to interrupt enemy attacks safely.

Ranked Play

Stay calm and focus on improving, not just winning.

Scythe Reads

Scythe relies heavily on predicting movement.

Spacing

Keep safe distance while controlling the stage.

Signature Control

Do not spam signatures against skilled players.

Team Combos

Coordinate attacks with teammates in 2v2 matches.

Map Awareness

Watch weapon spawns and enemy positioning carefully.

Training Mode

Use hitboxes and frame step tools to improve.

Legend Choice

Pick legends that match your playstyle naturally.

Practice Daily

Consistency is the fastest way to improve skill.
