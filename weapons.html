Brawlhalla Weapons

Each weapon in Brawlhalla has its own strengths, weaknesses, combos, and playstyle. Learning how each weapon works helps players improve their movement, spacing, and damage output.

Sword

The Sword is a light, single-handed blade and is one of the most balanced weapons in Brawlhalla. It has fast attacks, simple combos, and reliable kill options. It is great for beginners and experienced players because of its consistency and safety. While the basic moves stay the same for every legend, each character has different signature attacks, meaning every Sword legend plays differently.

Spear

The Spear has long range and strong spacing tools. It allows players to control distance and poke safely. Spear users can pressure opponents with quick light attacks and powerful signature moves.

Hammer

The Hammer is a heavy-hitting weapon with strong knockout power. Although slower than other weapons, it deals massive damage and can KO opponents early with well-timed hits.

Bow

The Bow is a fast, flexible weapon with great aerial control. It allows players to pressure from a distance and follow up with quick combos. Bow users can mix long-range pokes with fast close-range attacks.

Katars

Katars are fast, close-range weapons focused on strings and pressure. They allow players to stay close to opponents and deal continuous damage with quick attacks and mix-ups.
