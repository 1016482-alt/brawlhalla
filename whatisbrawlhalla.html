What is Brawlhalla

Brawlhalla is a free-to-play fighting game where players battle on different stages using unique legends, weapons, and fast-paced combat mechanics.. Each legend has unique weapons and abilities, making every match feel different and exciting.

The goal of the game is to knock your opponents off the stage. The more damage a player takes, the further they fly when hit. Players can choose from many game modes, including 1v1, 2v2, free-for-all, and custom lobbies with friends.

Many players enjoy Brawlhalla because it is easy to learn but hard to master. The game has regular updates, new legends, and fun events that keep the experience fresh for both new and experienced players.
